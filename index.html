About ViewTube AR OS

ViewTube AR OS is an augmented‑reality YouTube viewer designed for wearable devices and spatial interfaces. It allows users to securely sign in with their Google account and view their personal YouTube content in a floating, holographic AR environment.

Features

  • View your YouTube playlists
  • Browse playlist items
  • See your subscriptions
  • Access your liked videos
  • View your watch history
  • Channel pages and metadata
  • Floating AR video player
  • Gesture‑optimized UI

Privacy & Data Use

ViewTube AR OS is fully read‑only and does not upload, modify, or store any user data. All data is processed locally on your device and is only accessed with your explicit permission through Google OAuth.
