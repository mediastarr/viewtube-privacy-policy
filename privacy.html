Privacy Policy — ViewTube AR OS Last updated: June 2026 1. Introduction ViewTube AR OS (“the App”) is an augmented‑reality YouTube viewer that allows users to sign in with their Google account and access their own YouTube content in a read‑only format. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected. Contact: mediastarr.dev@gmail.com 2. Information We Access The App only accesses information you explicitly grant permission for through Google OAuth. The App does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data with any third parties. 2.1 YouTube Data (via youtube.readonly) Playlists Playlist items Subscriptions Liked videos Watch history Channel metadata All data is read‑only. 2.2 Google Profile Information (via userinfo.profile) Display name Profile photo 2.3 Google Account Email (via userinfo.email) Email address 2.4 Identity Verification (via openid) Unique identifier for login session 3. How We Use Your Information The App uses your Google data only to display your YouTube content inside the ViewTube AR OS interface. The App does not upload, modify, or delete any user data. 4. Data Storage The App does not store personal data on external servers. All data is processed locally and discarded when the session ends. 5. Data Sharing The App does not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. 6. Revoking Access You may revoke access at any time at: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions (myaccount.google.com in Bing) 7. Children’s Privacy Not intended for children under 13. 8. Changes to This Policy Updates may occur; the “Last updated” date will reflect changes. 9. Contact Information mediastarr.dev@gmail.com
