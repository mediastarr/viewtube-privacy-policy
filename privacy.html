1. Introduction ViewTube AR OS (“the App”) is an augmented‑reality YouTube viewer that allows users to sign in with their Google account and access their own YouTube content in a read‑only format. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected. If you have any questions, you may contact the developer at: mediastarr.dev@gmail.com 2. Information We Access The App only accesses information that you explicitly grant permission for through Google OAuth. The App does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data with any third parties. The App may access the following Google data: 2.1 YouTube Data (via youtube.readonly) Your playlists Playlist items Subscriptions Liked videos Watch history Your YouTube channel name and basic metadata This data is read‑only and cannot be modified by the App. 2.2 Google Profile Information (via userinfo.profile) Your Google display name Your Google profile photo 2.3 Google Account Email (via userinfo.email) Your Google account email address 2.4 Identity Verification (via openid) A unique identifier used only to maintain your login session 3. How We Use Your Information The App uses your Google data only to display your YouTube content inside the ViewTube AR OS interface. Specifically: Your playlists are shown in the Playlists tab Your subscriptions are shown in the Subscriptions tab Your profile picture, name, and email appear in the Account tab Your watch history and liked videos (if enabled) are displayed in their respective tabs The App does not: Upload videos Modify playlists Delete content Post on your behalf Access your Gmail Access any non‑YouTube Google data 4. Data Storage The App does not store any personal data on external servers. ✔ All data is processed in real time ✔ No data is saved after you close the App ✔ No data is transmitted to any third party Your access token is stored locally on your device only for the duration of your session. 5. Data Sharing The App does not share, sell, or transfer your data to: Advertisers Analytics companies Third‑party services External servers Your data stays on your device. 6. Revoking Access You may revoke the App’s access to your Google account at any time by visiting: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions (myaccount.google.com in Bing) Once revoked: The App loses access immediately No further data can be retrieved Your session ends 7. Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from children. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent version. 9. Contact Information If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact: mediastarr.dev@gmail.com
